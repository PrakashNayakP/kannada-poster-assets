Privacy Policy

Belaku – Kannada Poster Maker  ·  App ID: com.pnp.kannadapostermaker
Effective date: 19 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Belaku – Kannada Poster Maker mobile application (the "App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. We keep things simple: the App does not require you to create an account or sign in, and the posters and photos you work with stay on your device unless you choose to share or save them.

In short: No sign-up. Your photos and posters are processed on your device and are never uploaded to us. We use a few standard Google services (ads, content delivery, notifications, crash-free tooling) that may process limited device data as described below.

1. Information the App handles

  • Photos and posters you create. Images you pick from your gallery, capture with the camera, or create in the editor are stored only in the App's private storage on your device. They are never sent to our servers. They leave your device only if you explicitly save them to your gallery or share them to another app.
  • No personal profile. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, or location, and we do not maintain user accounts.

2. Third-party services we use

The App relies on the following Google services, which may collect or process limited data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising). Shows ads and may access a device advertising identifier and general device/usage information to serve and measure ads, including limiting repeated or fraudulent ad views. See How Google uses advertising data. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings.
  • Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore). Used to fetch the App's content — stickers, templates, quotes, designs and configuration — from our catalog. This is a read-only download of content to your device; we do not upload your personal data through it.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications). If you allow notifications, Google issues a device messaging token so we can send announcements (such as new designs or festival templates). You can turn notifications off any time in your device settings.
  • Google Play services, including on-device ML Kit (used for the "Cutout" background-removal feature — this runs entirely on your device and your photos are not uploaded) and the Google Play In-App Review prompt.

Google's handling of any data collected through these services is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Device permissions

  • Photos / storage — to let you pick an image and to save finished posters to your gallery.
  • Camera — only if you choose to capture a photo for a poster.
  • Notifications — to show optional announcements (you are asked, and can decline).
  • Internet — to download content and show ads.

4. How information is used

  • To let you create, edit, save and share posters.
  • To download and display the App's content catalog.
  • To show and measure ads that support the App.
  • To send optional notifications you have opted into.

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Children

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data retention & security

Posters and photos remain on your device until you delete them (or uninstall the App). We do not store your images on our servers. Data processed by the Google services above is retained per Google's policies.

7. Your choices

  • Reset or limit ad personalization via your device's advertising-ID settings.
  • Turn notifications on or off in your device settings.
  • Delete your saved posters from within the App or your gallery at any time.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
